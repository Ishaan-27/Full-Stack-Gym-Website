Sauna

Walking into a gym sauna can be an intimidating experience.Saunas are a wonderful place to relax and loosen up after an intense workout. The steam can help you to sweat out toxins, the heat will drain the tension from your muscles, and the quiet will be wonderfully restful.Spending time in the sauna might have a positive effect on heart health. Some research has shown that the high temperature exposure helps blood vessels expand, which helps improve circulation and lowers blood pressure.

Heated Swimming Pool

Swimming reduces stress as it has a soothing effect and exercising in water is easier on joints as you are buoyant in the water, this puts less stress on the joints and reduces the risk of injury. Our pools are also a great tool for rehabilitation programs. Our pools are heated to approximately 27 degrees, making it the perfect temperature for cold or hot days. Each swimming pool is used for an array of activities, including swimming laps, synchronized swimming, and water aerobics.
